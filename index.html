Meet Miss. Rozi

Rozi is a Counseling Psychologist with a postgraduate degree in psychology and extensive training in guidance and counseling. As a certified hypnotherapist, she offers a diverse range of skills to support individuals in their mental health journeys.

Rozi specializes in providing exceptional care and tailored treatment plans for children, adolescents, and adults. Her expertise includes psychometric testing, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT), and Hypnotherapy. She is proficient in conducting mental status examinations, taking detailed case histories, and developing effective interventions.

Her approach is compassionate and solution-oriented, focusing on building strong, trusting relationships with clients. Recognized for her ability to motivate, communicate, and manage diverse personalities, Rozi excels in diffusing stressful situations and achieving challenging goals.

Rozi is passionate about making a positive impact in the field of clinical psychology and looks forward to supporting you on your path to mental wellness.
