About us

Step into a realm where understanding meets transformation, where every challenge is met with empathy and expertise. Welcome to Well Vichaar, your sanctuary for mental wellness and personal growth. At Well Vichaar, we don't just listen; we guide, empower, and uplift. Led by a compassionate team of RCI Certified Clinical psychologists, counseling psychologists, and top-tier psychiatrists, we offer a haven for those seeking clarity, healing, and renewal. Our mission is simple yet profound: to help you not only find happiness but also build a life of deep satisfaction and purpose. Whether you're grappling with grief, wrestling with guilt, or battling fears and phobias, we're here to support you every step of the way. Discover the transformative power of positive psychology, hypnotherapy, and evidence-based therapies tailored to your unique needs. Let us be your partners in growth, your champions of resilience, and your guides to a brighter, more fulfilling tomorrow. Take the first step towards a life filled with joy, meaning, and liberation. At Well Vichaar, we believe in nurturing the next generation of mental health professionals. That's why we offer internship programs in various therapeutic modalities such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT), Hypnotherapy, Expressive Art Therapy, and more. Our interns receive comprehensive training and hands-on experience under the guidance of our seasoned team of psychologists and psychiatrists. Additionally, we provide project services including synopsis, research paper, dissertation, and thesis writing, ensuring that our clients receive expert assistance in their academic pursuits. Join us in shaping the future of mental wellness while advancing your career or academic goals.
