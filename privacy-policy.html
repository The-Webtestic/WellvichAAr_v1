PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED MAY 2024

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information for therapy purposes only.

2. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

3. Confidentiality

Your information is kept confidential, except as required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary and securely dispose of it afterward.

We do not share your information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your information.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

LAST UPDATED MAY 2024

1. Confidentiality

Your information is strictly confidential except in cases of harm to self or others.

2. Appointments

Sessions must be booked at least 2 days in advance. Clients are required to notify us at least 24 hours in advance for cancellations or rescheduling. While refunds will not be provided for cancellations, clients can reschedule their appointments if needed.

3. Payment

We accept payment only via credit/debit cards or electronic funds transfer at the time of the session.

4. Therapeutic Relationship

We work together with respect and trust towards agreed-upon goals.

5. Boundaries

We maintain professional boundaries and avoid dual relationships.

6. Emergencies

Seek immediate assistance in emergencies; we can provide support and resources.

7. Termination

Either party can end therapy at any time; referrals will be provided if needed.

8. Records

Records are securely maintained; clients can access them upon request.
